3.4
---

1. Do not reveal the goal of your presentation.
   - __False__. Otherwise it would be unstructured and/or not interesting.
2. Help the audience to realise the usefullness of your presentation.
   - __True__. Again, in order to make it interesting.
3. Make your audience feel comfortable.
   - __True__. This helps them to focus on the topic.
4. Do _not_ ask your audience questions.
   - __False__. Questions help to clarify points of presentation and
     to check the audience's understanding.
5. Say who you are and what you do.
   - __True__. It helps to clarify your relation to the topic.
6. All jokes can be insulting.
   - __False__. While technically any joke can be considered such,
     more careful ones can help you to keep your audience interested.
